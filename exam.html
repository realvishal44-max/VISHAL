📊 Exam Pattern

BCA Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution (Simple Explanation)

📝

Section 1: General Exam Pattern (For All Semesters)

Before attempting exams, you should understand these basic rules:

  • Total BCA Marks: The complete BCA course carries a total of 3200 marks.
  • Question Paper Style: In each exam, there will be 10 long-answer type questions.
  • Attempt Rule: You need to attempt any 5 questions of your choice.
  • Equal Marks: All 5 questions carry 16 marks.
  • Time Duration: Each theory and practical exam is conducted for 3 hours.

Marks Division (Sem 1–5):

20 Internal Exam
80 External Exam
100 Total per Subject
