Information We Collect

At BCA STORE, we believe in minimal data collection to ensure a safe and private environment for our students.

  • Basic account information provided during login.
  • User feedback, suggestions, and support messages.
  • Platform usage data (anonymous) for improving performance and stability.

How Information Is Used

The information we collect is used solely to enhance your academic experience:

  • To improve platform features and user experience.
  • To maintain website stability and technical security.
  • To respond to feedback, bug reports, and support requests.
  • To prevent misuse, spam activity, or unauthorized access.

Data Protection

We take security seriously and implement standard measures to protect your digital footprint:

  • We do not intentionally sell or share personal user information with unauthorized third parties.
  • Reasonable security measures are used to protect user data from unauthorized access.
